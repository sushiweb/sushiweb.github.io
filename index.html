Each dish is a delicious dish from our sushi chefs. All of the freshest ingredients.

Since 2001, Ocean Sushi has been offering a selection of innovative twists to the classics of Japanese cuisine. Delicacies range from a variety of flavored snacks, top-notch sushi and sashimi.

Sushi Ocean was shown on the NTV television channel as one of the best restaurants. In addition, the Sushi Ocean is known to be a “meeting place for celebrities,” and has been seen in various magazines.

For lunch, this is a great place to have a quick bite to eat or just relax and chat with friends.

For supper, this is a trendy, fun and noisy environment where you can have fun in advance with friends before an evening walk. We also show all your favorite sports on 3 big screen TVs.
